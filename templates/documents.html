DEPARTURE INSTRUCTIONS

Welcome to the Siberian cat world!
SAFE HEAVEN

When bringing home a new kitten, expect it to be nervous. Provide a small room with food, water, and litter box separated. Build trust gradually over the first few days.

NIBBLING

Nibbling should not be permitted. Redirect to toys and use firm "NO!" if needed. Do not scruff kittens older than 8 months.

FOOD & CARE

Feed 3x daily with high-quality kitten food. No cow's milk or people food. Regular vet visits and vaccinations required.
