About Our Farm

RocKaRan Cat Farm specializes in breeding beautiful, healthy Siberian cats. Our cats are raised in a loving family environment and are perfect companions for families with allergies due to their hypoallergenic qualities.

We focus on health, temperament, and socialization to ensure each kitten becomes a wonderful addition to your family.

Why Siberian Cats?

Hypoallergenic

Lower levels of Fel d 1 protein make them suitable for many allergy sufferers.

Gentle Nature

Known for their calm, affectionate temperament and love for families.

Beautiful Coats

Stunning semi-long coats in various colors and patterns.

Healthy Breed

Hardy, robust cats with excellent health and longevity.
